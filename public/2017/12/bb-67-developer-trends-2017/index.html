BB 67: Developer Trends 2017

December 1, 2017 in survey, developer-survey


Welcome to episode BB67 of Bangalore Bits. In today’s show we are going to revisit stack exchange developer survey published in march 2017.

For past five years or so Stack Exchange has been conducting these detailed survey by sending out bunch of questioner to various software developer across the world who are working in very diverse environments. Apart from analysing the responses they were also taking into consideration of the developer contributions on their properties as well.

The high level objectives of this survey has been to identify industry trends, technology trends, diversity, most loved & dreaded technologies as well compensations etc.,

As we are approaching end-of 2017 its probably a good time to revisit the findings and see what still holds good, what are some of those new skills, technologies, languages we are going to see as inclusion in 2018.

How automation and Machine Learning is going to change software echo system forever.
